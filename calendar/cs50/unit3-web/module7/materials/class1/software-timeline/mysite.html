A Timeline of Beloved (and at Times, Embarrassing) Memories

As an individual born in the early 1980's, computer software played an integral part in my personal development. In fact, for a brief glimmer in time, it almost seemed as though the techonological capabilities were growing developing at the same rate as myself and my peers. As early as kindergarten, we relied upon classroom computers to teach us the basics, such as math, reading, and typing. Looking back at some of the software we cherished is a bit like flipping through a family photo album. Take a walk down Memory Lane, and marvel at the progress made in just three-and-a-half decades.
